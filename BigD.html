Alpha1

I am proposing an idea to build an online university to provide educational opportunities for more Burmese youth and help bridge the gap in their education. The current political climate in Myanmar has brought about a revolution throughout the country, and as a result, the majority of students, approximately 80%, are unable to continue their academic pursuits. I am determined to utilize the knowledge and skills that I have gained during my seven semesters of study abroad to build this online university, despite potential obstacles. The development and implementation of this online university has the potential to provide access to education for many individuals who may otherwise be unable to continue their studies due to the current political climate.

In this link, We are providing a link to a BBC News website where readers can find updated information on the current political and social conditions in Myanmar. We have received permission from the BBC to share this link in order to raise awareness and understanding of the situation in the country.
