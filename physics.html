Kinematics

Concepts Description
Kinematics The study of motion without considering the forces that cause the motion
Displacement A vector quantity that represents the change in position of an object
Velocity A vector quantity that represents the rate of change of displacement
Acceleration A vector quantity that represents the rate of change of velocity
Distance A scalar quantity that represents the total length of the path traveled by an object
Time A scalar quantity that is used to measure the duration of an event
Speed A scalar quantity that represents the rate at which an object moves
Motion graphs Graphical representations of an object's motion
Free-fall motion The motion of an object under the influence of gravity alone, without considering air resistance
Projectile motion The motion of an object projected into the air and subjected to the force of gravity
Equations Description
Δx = x_f - x_i Displacement
v = Δx / t Velocity
a = Δv / t Acceleration
d = vt Distance
(1/2) * a * t^2 Distance traveled in Free-fall motion
v = at Final velocity in Free-fall motion
v^2 = v_i^2 + 2 * a * d Final velocity in Free-fall motion
